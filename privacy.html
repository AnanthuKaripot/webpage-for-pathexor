Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

PG PathScheduler ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. We adhere to the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Data

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. We strictly collect only:

  • Basic Profile Info: Name, Email Address, and Profile Picture (provided by Google).
  • User ID: A unique identifier to link your data.
  • Guest Mode: If you choose "Continue as Guest", we do not collect your profile data. All study data created in Guest Mode is associated with an anonymous ID.

B. User-Generated Content

To provide the core functionality, we process data you create:

  • Study Data: Timetables, completion status, and study streaks.
  • Learning Data: Flashcard progress (FSRS), Quiz scores, and Bookmarks.
  • Notes & Media: Personal notes, Voice recordings, and uploaded diagrams.

C. Device & Usage Data

  • Local Storage: Progress is stored locally on your device (SQLite). You are responsible for securing your physical device.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics for pseudonymous usage data (screen views, crash reports).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To sync your notes and progress across multiple devices.
  • Personalization: To calculate Rank Prediction and schedule spacing for Flashcards.
  • Communication: To send critical account updates.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

  • No Sale of Data: We do NOT sell or rent your personal information.
  • Third-Party Services: Auth (Google), Database (Firestore), AI (Gemini API for explaining concepts), and OCR (ML Kit - on-device).
  • Safety Reporting: Reported AI responses are collected solely to improve safety and accuracy.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account permanently via Settings > Delete Account. Data is irretrievably erased after a 30-day recovery window.
